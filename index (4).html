Technology

What is an Archaeological Prediction Model?

Think of it as a smart map that reads the landscape like an archaeologist. Instead of spending years walking fields and digging randomly, the APM analyzes thousands of environmental variables to tell you: here is where people lived.

🏔 Topographic Analysis

Elevation, slope and terrain relief — ancient settlements preferred defensible high ground and gentle southern slopes close to resources.

💧 Hydrographic Proximity

Distance to rivers, springs and wetlands — every civilization in history settled within reach of fresh water. The model maps those ancient corridors.

🪨 Geological Conditions

Soil fertility, substrate stability and mineral availability — factors that drove human migration and permanent settlement for 10,000 years.

Process

How DetectLab Works

01

Data Collection

We aggregate topographic, hydrographic and geological datasets from national and international sources.

02

QGIS Modeling

Layers are processed and weighted inside QGIS using spatial algorithms refined through years of archaeological fieldwork.

03

Scoring & Classification

Every zone receives an APM score from 1.2 to 5.0 representing its probability of containing buried archaeological remains.

04

Interactive Map

Results are published as an interactive web map overlaid on satellite imagery — ready for researchers, institutions and enthusiasts.
